Privacy & Data Security

At Pleasure Print Studios, we value your privacy as much as your creativity. Your trust is the foundation of our studio.

1. Data Collection

We only collect information necessary to process your orders, facilitate custom STL requests, or communicate regarding wholesale inquiries. This may include your name, email address, and shipping details.

2. Data Usage

We do not sell your data. Period. Your information is used strictly for fulfilling your requests and improving our service.

3. Analytics & Cookies

We use Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager to understand how visitors interact with our site. This helps us see which designs are popular so we can create more of what you love. By using our site, you agree to our use of cookies for these purposes.

4. Intellectual Property

For custom STL commissions, we treat your uploaded reference files with strict confidentiality. We do not share or resell custom designs submitted by our clients unless explicitly agreed upon.
