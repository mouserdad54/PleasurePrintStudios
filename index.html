Keeping it Peach Perfect

Your gear is 3D printed with precision. Follow these steps to keep it looking and feeling brand new.

🧼

Cleaning

Use lukewarm water and mild dish soap. Wipe gently with a soft microfiber cloth.

☀️

Temperature Control

Avoid environments exceeding 130°F. High heat will cause the geometry to warp.

🚫

Chemicals

Avoid harsh alcohol-based cleaners. Stick to skin-safe soaps.

🧴

Compatibility

We recommend using a water-based lubricant if play involves surface contact.

Common Questions about PPS Gear

What makes 3D printed impact toys different?

We use Bambu PLA Tough Plus for higher impact resistance than standard prints.

Can I request a custom STL file?

Yes! We offer custom STL modeling services for home printing or studio production.
