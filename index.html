Common Questions about PPS Gear

What makes 3D printed impact toys different?

Unlike traditional materials, our 3D printed gear is designed for a specific aesthetic and durability balance. We use Bambu PLA Tough Plus, which offers higher impact resistance than standard 3D prints, making our paddles perfect for both display and use.

Can I request a custom STL file for my own 3D printer?

Yes! We offer custom STL modeling services. If you have a unique vision for a 3D design, our studio can create the digital file for you to print at home, or we can handle the high-quality production for you.

Do you offer wholesale pricing for boutiques?

Absolutely. Pleasure Print Studios specializes in wholesale 3D printing. We provide bulk inventory and private labeling for shops looking to add a kawaii, high-impact aesthetic to their collection.

How do I clean my Pleasure Print Studios gear?

To maintain the 3D printed finish, simply wipe with lukewarm water and mild soap. Avoid high heat (like dishwashers) as it can warp the precision-printed PLA material.
